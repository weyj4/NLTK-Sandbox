About

This simple Flask app is an effort to expose some of the functionality of Python's Natural Language Toolkit (NLTK) through an easy-to-use interface. The idea is to provide nontechnical people who are curious about machine learning and natural language processing with a way to experiment and learn. That said, NLTK comes with a great free tutorial that forms the basis of most of the material here, and even someone with little or no programming experience could, with a little commitment, master the basics of Python and the NLTK to great effect.
